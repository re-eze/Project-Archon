Geo Archon - Zhongli

About the Geo Archon

Zhongli was a god well before The Seven even existed and one of the oldest still living in Teyvat. He knows much about Liyue history and culture in part due to his time as the Geo Archon and still fulfill his first contract.

Zhongli's Characteristics

  1. A mysterious expert contracted by the Wangsheng Funeral
  2. Extremely knowledgable in all things
  3. Calm, reserved and polite a man
