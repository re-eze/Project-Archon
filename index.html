Geo Archon - Zhongli

About the Geo Archon

Zhongli was a god well before The Seven even existed and one of the oldest still living in Teyvat. He is said to have a stoic demeanor for duty, Zhongli also has a sentimental side. He knows much about Liyue history and culture in part due to his time as the Geo Archon.

Zhongli's Characteristics

  1. A mysterious expert contracted by the Wangsheng Funeral
  2. Extremely knowledgable in all things
  3. Calm, reserved and polite a man
